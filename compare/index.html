How scores are calculated

Every product is scored 0-10 on five criteria: stability, cushioning, wide-foot friendliness, value, and durability. These scores are derived directly from the reasoning in each product's full review, not a separate independent rating — see the shoe database for the full methodology and to browse every product at once.
