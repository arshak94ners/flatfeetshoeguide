How this is different from just browsing the guides

Our category guides lay out every pick with full reasoning, which is the right format if you want to compare options yourself. This quiz is the shortcut version — it applies the same decision logic from those guides (activity, arch severity, width, budget) and lands on one specific answer instead of a list. What this quiz doesn't do

It only recommends products we've actually reviewed on this site — currently 15 shoes and insoles across running, walking, work, gym, sandals, and insoles. It's not scanning the entire shoe market, and it's not a substitute for a podiatrist if you have persistent pain. See our medical disclaimer for more on that distinction.

Frequently asked questions

How does the shoe finder quiz work?

It asks a few questions about how you'll use the shoe (running, walking, work, gym, sandals, or insoles), your arch severity, width, and budget, then matches your answers to a specific product from the shoes and insoles we've already reviewed on this site. It only recommends products from our existing catalog, not the entire market.

Does the quiz recommend products because of affiliate commissions?

The recommendation logic is based on the same reasoning in our written reviews and comparisons — support level, activity, and fit — not which product pays a higher commission. We do use Amazon affiliate links on the resulting recommendations, disclosed here and in our affiliate disclosure.

What if none of the results feel right for me?

The quiz only covers the products we've reviewed so far, which is a curated list, not the whole market. If a result doesn't feel like a fit, our full category guides cover more context and alternatives, and you're welcome to contact us with specifics.
